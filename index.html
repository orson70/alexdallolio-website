Selected Work Get in touch
Who this is for

For companies with strong products, rich material and a video narrative that does not yet match their value.

Factories, archives, case studies, technical processes, events, products, buildings, prototypes, old footage. I work with what already exists and turn it into films for websites, fairs, presentations, campaigns and internal launches.

The Approach

A tailor who works
with any fabric.

Most productions begin with a location, a crew and a shooting schedule. Mine often begins with what a company already has: archive footage, product images, technical material, renders, photographs, fragments.

Direction is not only what happens on set. It is the ability to see structure, rhythm, tension and meaning inside raw material.

No shoot required

I work remotely with existing assets. No travel, no production days, no location permits.

AI-native workflow

Generative AI is used as part of the visual process: to extend, reframe, imagine and build what the archive cannot provide.

Director's eye

Concept, editing, rhythm, music and visual treatment turn scattered material into something people can actually watch.

Works anywhere

Based in Milan, working globally. If the material exists, geography matters less.
